About WithUs

WithUs was started with mission: to raise awareness and end the stigma of mental health.

Who are we

Think of WithUs as your mind's best friend. We're here for you whenever you need us, wherever you are, helping you get through tough times and find joy in every day.

What we do

Through science-backed meditation end mindfulness tools, WithUs helps you create life-changing habits to support your mental health and find a healthier, happier you.

Why we do

WithUs is proven to reduce stress by 14% in just 10 days. It can also help you relax your mind in minutes, improve focus, and get the best sleep ever.
